Geomemo

Memory Card Game

How to play

The game begins with all cards face-down.

You can flip over two cards at a time on every turn to reveal their faces. Click or select the cards to flip them.

If the two cards have matching pictures, you have found a pair! The matched cards are left face-up in the playing area.

If the two cards do not match, you must remember the locations and pictures of the cards. The cards will be flipped face down again, and you can start your next turn.

Continue flipping over two cards at a time, trying to find all the matching pairs.

The game is won when all the cards have been matched and removed from the playing area.

You have 60 seconds to find all matches. The game is lost if you cannot find all matches during the 60-second timeframe.
